About Our Previous Books

Here we have listed all of our previous books so you can see what kind of books we have read in the past to see if your reading prefrences match what we read for the club. We have also done this so newer members can see our old books if they enjoy what we read and want more books like those. Each book has at least one link that will take you to a site where you can purchase the book in question.

All the Light We Cannot See by Anthony Doerr

This book tells the stories of two individuals during the Nazi time period that vary greatly yet later converge. This was a very well written story which lead to everyone enjoying this read very much. Overall the club gave this book a 9.5/10.

Image Source: All the light we cannot see: A novel: Doerr, Anthony. (n.d.). Retrieved December 3, 2022, from https://www.amazon.com/All-Light-We-Cannot-See/dp/1501173219

City of Bones by Cassandra Clare

As the first book in The Mortal Instruments series, this book introduces you to the Shadowhunter world by following the main character, Clary Fray, as she gets thrown head first into the Shadow World. The club enjoyed this book and overall gave it a 9/10.

Image source: City of Bones (the mortal instruments book 1) - amazon.com. (n.d.). Retrieved December 3, 2022, from https://www.amazon.com/City-Bones-Mortal-Instruments-Book-ebook/dp/B0013TXA5Y

Moby Dick by Herman Melville

Moby Dick is the classic story told from the perspective of a sailor that is under a ship's captain that wants revenge on the titular whale Moby Dick. This classic was not a big hit with our club members they ended up giving it a 5/10.
